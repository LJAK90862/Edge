01 — Identity
The logo

The Edge Energy mark is built from three layered chevrons graduating from deep forest green to bright mint — representing momentum, clarity, and forward motion. Always use the logo at the correct minimum size and never alter its proportions.

Primary — Light
Primary — Dark
Primary — Green
Icon — Favicon/App
Horizontal — Full Lockup with Tagline

✓ Do

  • Use the logo on white, dark slate, or forest green backgrounds
  • Maintain clear space equal to the height of the 'e' around all sides
  • Use the icon-only mark for favicons, app icons, and small spaces
  • Use the full lockup when space allows

✗ Don't

  • Stretch, rotate, or alter the logo proportions
  • Place on busy photographic backgrounds without a backing
  • Recreate the mark — always use the provided SVG files
  • Change the gradient colours or use flat fills on the chevron mark

04 — Voice & Tone
How we speak

Edge Energy speaks like a trusted advisor, not a salesperson. Direct, plain, and always honest.

Confident, not arrogant

"We'll tell you if your current contract is already competitive. We'd rather lose a deal than waste your time."

Plain, not dumbed down

"We search the whole market, negotiate with suppliers, and handle the switch from start to finish."

Human, not corporate

"A real person in the UK handles your account. No call centres. No bots."

Honest, not salesy

"Independent, whole-of-market, and always working in your interest." — Never "best deal guaranteed".

The tagline
The Edge in Business Energy.
Use in full on letterheads, email signatures, and footer. Use abbreviated "edge energy" in navigation and compact contexts. Always lowercase.
