about us

we started because we were
tired of the tricks

founded in london by former energy industry professionals who'd seen enough. inflated rates, opaque pricing, suppliers rewarding brokers for steering clients the wrong way. we knew technology could fix it — so we built edge energy.

the energy market moves constantly — rates shift, supplier positions change, new products appear. most businesses only look at it at renewal. our platform monitors it every day, using data and intelligent automation to keep our clients ahead.

we built our own deal room platform because the industry still runs on spreadsheets and phone calls. from digital letter of authority to automated supplier comparison to in-app contract signing — we've digitised the entire switching process.

  • we compare the same tariffs your direct supplier would offer you
  • our software monitors suppliers across the market and analyses rates in real time
  • a dedicated account manager handles your account from day one
  • automated renewal tracking ensures you never overpay again
