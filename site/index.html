Why we're different

The brokers who actually
have your back

Most energy brokers work for the supplier. We work for you. Independent, whole-of-market, and focused entirely on getting your business the best deal available.

🔍

Whole-of-market search

We compare every major UK energy supplier — not just a preferred panel — so you get the genuine best deal available, not the best deal we're incentivised to sell.

🤝

Dedicated account manager

A real person in the UK handles your switch from start to finish. No call centres, no bots — just straight-talking support from someone who knows your account.

📋

Plain-English contracts

We go through every term before you sign. Auto-renewal clauses? Exit fees? We flag everything before it becomes a problem — not after.

♻️

Green options available

Want 100% renewable? We'll find you a genuinely certified green tariff and verify the REGO certificates behind it — no greenwashing, no vague claims.

Renewal reminders

We track your contract end date and contact you 90, 60, and 30 days before expiry — so you never roll onto an out-of-contract rate by accident.

Multi-site portfolios

Managing multiple meters? We handle the whole portfolio — one contact, consolidated reporting, and a single renewal calendar across all your sites.

About us

We started because we were
tired of the tricks

Founded in London, Edge Energy was built by former energy industry professionals who'd seen enough. Inflated rates, opaque pricing, suppliers rewarding brokers for steering clients the wrong way. So we left and built something better.

The energy market moves constantly — rates shift, supplier positions change, new products come to market. Most businesses only look at it at renewal. We look at it every day. That's the edge we bring.

  • We compare the same tariffs your direct supplier would offer you.
  • We work to save you money long-term, not to close a quick deal.
  • We serve SMEs, charities, schools, landlords, and multi-site businesses across the UK.
  • A dedicated account manager handles your account from day one.
